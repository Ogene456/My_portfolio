EDUCATIONAL BACKGROUND

Ogene Joan Omotineh started her educational pursuit at Depowa Day Care Nursery and Primary School,Mogadishu cantonement,Asokoro-Abuja
where she completed her nursery and early primary education. She later moved to Aja Ultra Modern Nursery and Primary School,Karu,Nassarawa
state for her second year of primary education. In search of completing her primary education,Joan attended Naowa Model Nursery and Primary
School,Asokoro-Abuja where she finally completed her primary education. Furthermore,Joan also attended City Royal Secondary School,Nyanya-Abuja
where she did her complete secondary education. Miss Ogene is currently studying Medicine and Surgery at the University of Jos,Jos-Plateau state.
